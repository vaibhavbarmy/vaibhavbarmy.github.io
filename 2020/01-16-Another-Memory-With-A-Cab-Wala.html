Another Memory With A Cab Wala

Thursday, January 16th 2020, 10:14 pm

Jo malik aapke kaam ko nei samajh sakta, vo kabhi aapko nei samajh sakta.

Aapko ab our job nei chodni chahiye, recession sir pe hai.

He has 13000 payment pending with his last employer but shows no interest in taking it kyunki agar jayega to Malik phuchega kyun choda, choda kyunki malik daarubaaj tha.

So shifting recently to Gurgaon again, I had to go to my mama’s home for weekends.

Like always (When I don’t ride my bike), took Sawari cab to reach Faridabad. While going to faridabad I met a peculiar cab wala.

He was 31yrs of age, young in his life he has a very rare attitude. He had worked for a lot of companies as driver and is currently working as driver for logistic company.

So below are the highlights of my conversation with him :-

He takes great pride in the way by which he left his last job in 5 star hotel, his wife was pregnant and they were not giving him leaves for more than 10 days.
Aapki wife to gaav me rehti hai, aapko bharosa hai uspe? Sir bharose ke bina shaadi tikti nhi hai.
