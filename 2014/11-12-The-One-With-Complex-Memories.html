The One With Complex Memories

Wednesday, November 12th 2014, 11:44 pm

Boy thinking: One of the girls sure has a weird name Teacher: I heard today during recess u hit this another boy with basketball…

Ball flew towards that another boy who wasnt playing and just watching from the side ..

Boy and the girl with the weird name sitting together ..

Scene 1

LKG the results r out

Boy: (In an excited tone, pulling his dad’s shirt) dad, dad did I pass the class?

Father: Yes u did (with a half glance).

Now having a conversation with the teacher.

“Which student got the best result.”

Teacher: There r two girls who performed best.

Father: what’s their name

The teacher told both the names.

Boy thinking: One of the girls sure has a weird name

Scene 2

Class 1st: Everyone was doing something or the other…

Teacher: (Calling out the name) Who is this boy?

Boy: Yes mam

Teacher: I heard today during recess u hit this another boy (who was also standing) with basketball…

Boy remembering the incident… Everyone was chasing the basketball which was being used as a football to play .. Out of nowhere ball came towards the boy… Like a dog chasing a car who has just caught the car but don’t know what to do with it… He kicked it hard .. Ball flew towards that another boy who wasn’t playing and just watching from the side .. Hit him he fell over…

Coming back to class

Boy: Mam I didn’t hit him intentionally

Another boy: Mam he is lying.

Boy shocked of the accusation watching silently…

Teacher: Behave urself you are a slacker who just sleeps in the class .. (The other boy was the topper that year) whole class laughing

Boy thinking this is what it is to be the topper of the batch .. Obnoxious he is .. Why wud I play this cruel joke to a person who couldn’t play games

Scene 3

class 1st again…

Boy and the girl with the weird name sitting together .. Boy thinking she is also good in studies I should remain silent (don’t want to get in any trouble)

Girl: what’s ur name (asked in a friendly manner)

Few days after they r friends kind of

Boy and girl were discussing something which they both thought is beyond their reach of mind .. Amazed how they both can think abt it being small children they both r…

Boy: what do your parents call u at home

Girl: Telling the name used mostly by her family, adding my mom use to call me by this name… But I don’t like it at all .. Telling a second name which was a bit less weird .. I like this name more

Few weeks after both back to their usual routine, but the boy with a motivation to be like her

Scene 4

Class 2nd results day

Boy with his parents and the teacher who really treated him well

U came 2nd in class concerning performance

Boy thinking .. 2nd not even 3rd ..directly 2nd

Parents were asking who performed best… The same another boy

His heart fell… Was hoping to be sm1 else there

Scene 5

Time unknown

The boy was chasing the girl with the weird name after school wanting to ask something .. Girl didn’t like it… She stopped looked back and snapped .. Don’t follow me my dad is in police…

Boy at home asking both his parents: Is her dad in police?

Dad and mom surprised then mom replied

Of course not her dad isn’t in police…

Boy thinking… But then why did she lie .. Liar, she is

Next few years the resentment grew into competitive feeling between the boy and girl.

Scene 6

Class 6th

The girl with the weird name had to miss mid-term exams of two subjects coz of some function in her family .. When the results came she performed better than the boy in the rest of exams but overall she lost .. Boy watching her as she was distressed and was hiding her face on desk and appearing to others that she was sleeping ..

But he knew the better truth .. she was crying.

Scene 7

Around the end month of class 6 the

Boy gets to know she was leaving the school.
