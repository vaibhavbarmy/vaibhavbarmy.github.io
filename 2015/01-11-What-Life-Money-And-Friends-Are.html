What Life, Money And Friends Are?

Sunday, January 11th 2015, 12:51 am

Life me aisa kaam karna chahiye ki puri duniya tumhe jaane aur sadiyon tak yaad rakhe.

Tumne kuch aisa nahi kiya to bhi tumhe tumhari family aur bachhe kuch time tak yaad rakhenge after you die.

Excerpts of a conversation with one of my office mate while having a break.

Life

Life me aisa kaam karna chahiye ki puri duniya tumhe jaane aur sadiyon tak yaad rakhe.

Usse kya hoga?

Tumne kuch aisa nahi kiya to bhi tumhe tumhari family aur bachhe kuch time tak yaad rakhenge after you die.

Sadiyan bhi kabhi khatam ho jayegi, time is relative my friend kam time ke liye kam log yaad rakhe, ya zyada time ke liye zyada log yaad rakhe.

Outcome same hoga aek din, your name will be lost in the dust of history, Nobody can escape this cycle of time.

So why worry just do what you like to do and If it is written that many people will remember you for sadiyon tak, to badhiya hai nahi to koi tension nahi lene ka.

Money

Life me itna paisa hona chahiye ki jo tumne socha jab socha tab kar sako. If I want to go to the US today, I can go to.

Usse kya hoga?

Marna sabko hai aek na aek din ji ke marne ke liye paise nahi chahiye

Everyone live and die in this world from a cell to the extinct dinosaurs

The more complicated you let your mind and need become the more complex you make your life.

Take the example of a sloth, kya vo is baat se pareshan hai ki uski life kahan ja rhi hai?

He/She will die, you will die too.

outcome same hoga aek din.

So why worry just do what you would like to do in place of doing what you want to do, or you think you need to do.

Friend

Life me sabse achhe dost kaun hote hai, school ke? College ke? Ya uske baad ke?

Abhi ke dost
