And baby cockroaches were devoured and hunted by this winter even before they were able to grow.

Discussions on revenue rather than a 10x return based on user acquisition were what investors and startup pandits were looking for.

What it showed to young entrepreneurs was a lesson worth it,

That old saying of the customer is always right is in fact true.

That is treating your employees with respect and dignity is a must.

Treating people as people rather than machines is a must.

Excellence is something to thrive for rather than exposure and glamour.

That starting up is something more than a startup, something closer to running a business.

That early adopters of startup summers are as much flawed and imperfect as we are.

That an enterprise is for the people, by the people and its foundations are its building blocks.

That when life punches you hard on your face, you have to stay strong and pull your team up along with you.
