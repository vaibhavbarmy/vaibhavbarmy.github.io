A Memory With The Safai Wala

Tuesday, October 22nd 2019, 2:13 pm

So this one time my mom dad were fighting regarding money while we were travelling, same old shit and general struggles of middle class family.

So I told them am going for a walk for sometime and left their company/fight to take my mind off things.

Light a cigarette and think about the issue they were fighting on, however even after lighting cigarette I didn’t feel better.

So I came up with another solution lets burn the money I have in my wallet with fire.

Just I was about to do the above a thought crossed my mind, लक्ष्मी को कभी जलाया नहीं जाता!

So I stopped before executing my stupidity !!!

However I still wanted to get rid of that evil money I had, so I saw a सफाई वाला, went towards him and gave him that 20 INR bill which I was about to burn, while telling him the reason and the stupid act I was about to make !!!

The first thing he said after receiving and listening to me in a very happy manner, लक्ष्मी को कभी जलाया थोड़े नहीं जाता हैं, हमारे काम आ जाएगा, इस रुपैया की जरूरत हमें आपसे ज़्यादा हैं !!!

Surprised after hearing the same saying from him which stopped me from doing a stupid act, I gave him another 20 INR bill, asked his name, shook his hands and left !!!

I felt not just good but great after that !!! :) :)

While going back to my mom and dad I reflected on these facts :-

  • How there is a bigger wisdom in the crowd
  • How education doesn’t make you the most intelligent person in any room
  • How there is some form of wisdom in every single person on this mother earth
  • How there is strength in united wisdom more than any wisdom of flawed individual humans
  • How our country has kept their morals and wisdom of so many thousands years of ancient history
  • How even after being looted out of our resources there is still that same old wisdom present in each and every Indian
  • How good karma should be the ultimate destiny and journey for every person, country rather than anything else

Enough of how and now :P Lets celebrate happiness this awesome Deepawali !!!
