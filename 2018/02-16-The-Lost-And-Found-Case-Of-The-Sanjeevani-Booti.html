Real story

Those who follow Hinduism must already have heard of metaphysical importance behind folklore of Sanjeevani Booti.

Its a medicinal herb used by Ram Ji to save Lakshman Ji which was very bravely brought by Hanuman Ji (whole mountain).

While our reporter was going through the busy streets of Nehru Place, he found a guy selling a very curious plant.

The plant was dead and dry but when you dip it inside water for a few days all the dead leaves and roots regain their life without any exception.

You might be thinking this happens to every plant through photosynthesis, but there is a big difference when each leaf regains their life against new leaves being created by other kinds of similar plant.

While enquiring more about it, he got to know that the plant which he was selling with a different name is actually Sanjeevani Booti.

He had been selling it for the past 7 years, and since nobody used to believe him that it's Sanjeevi Booti, he stopped telling the real name of this plant.
It can be verified from google image search results that what he was saying is actually entirely accurate.

Like how its written in the folklore of Hinduism the plant has almost every imagined medical benefit to your body provided you eat its green leaves.

Our reporter took a photo of the guy selling Sanjeevani Boots inside Nehru Place of New Delhi.
